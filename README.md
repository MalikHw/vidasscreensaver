## how to use

1. open the app
2. hit **Choose Video** and pick any mp4 from your phone — or tap **Browse Catalog** to grab something pre-made
3. tweak playback stuff (sound, loop, how it scales)
4. hit **SAVE**
5. tap **Open System Daydream Settings** and set this app as your screensaver
6. plug in your phone and watch it go

### scale modes
- **Fit** — whole video visible, black bars if needed
- **Stretch** — fills screen, ratio goes bye bye
- **Zoom** — fills screen, crops the edges (default, looks the best imo)
- **Adapt** — same as fit but explicit black bars, great for vertical videos on a wide screen
